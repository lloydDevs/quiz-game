Game Mechanics

You will have 20 seconds to answer as many questions as possible. The game will display questions one at a time, and your goal is to answer each question correctly before moving to the next one.

The timer will start as soon as the first question is answered. Keep answering questions until the timer runs out. When the 20 seconds are up, your final score, which is the total number of correctly answered questions, will be displayed.

Challenge yourself to answer as many questions as you can and set a new high score!
